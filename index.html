About LUXE

LUXE is a fictional e-commerce platform dedicated to showcasing the art of modern luxury and timeless design. This site serves as a frontend development mockup, built purely with HTML, Tailwind CSS, and Vanilla JavaScript to demonstrate robust single-page application (SPA) behaviors, fluid transitions, and component reusability.

Our Philosophy

We believe that luxury should be accessible and defined by quality, not just price. Every product in our collection‚Äîthough simulated‚Äîis designed to represent sophistication, meticulous craftsmanship, and enduring style. We aim to inspire confidence and elegance in every piece.

Technology Stack

  • **HTML5:** Semantic structure.
  • **Tailwind CSS:** Utility-first styling for rapid, responsive design.
  • **Vanilla JavaScript:** Core logic, state management (using `localStorage`), and component rendering.
  • **SPA Simulation:** Page transitions are handled via dynamic class toggling and CSS animations, mimicking a true single-page application experience.
